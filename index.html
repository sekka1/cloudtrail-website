WHAT SECURITY ACCESS CONTROL POLICIES DO YOU HAVE?

Amazon AWS policies control what a user has access to. These are usually really detailed and long configuration statement meant for machines to read. It is almost impossible for a human to manually ensure these policies are correct.

WHEN WERE YOUR SECURITY POLICIES CHANGED?

Tired of being sherlock holmes when trying to figure out when your security policies were last changed, or when new access was created or deleted? We make it easy to find out when security policies have been modified and even send out a notification of when things change by monitoring your policies.

WHERE WERE YOUR SYSTEMS LAST ACCESSED FROM?

Do you know where your systems were last accessed? What was the last physical location that your systems were last accessed? Find out where people are logging into your systems from quickly and easily.

WHY WERE THE SYSTEMS ACCESSED?

Why were your systems last updated? Did any of your systems change? Find out why the systems were accessed in the first place by monitoring changes to your systems.

How?

We think we have the answer. Quickly monitor and see analytics around the who, what, when, where, and why for your cloud access controls

About Us

We are a small but nimble and diverse team with backgrounds in development operations, software engineering, infrastructure development, security auditing, and monitoring of security controls. We have built cloud infrastructures for large corporations and even small startups.

But we ran into a problem.

Every time we set up something new, we wanted simple metrics on basic things like who accessed what, when it was accessed, how it was accessed, and where it was accessed from. This generally required building custom tools or spending a ton of money. This is when we started creating a solution that was easy to use and could provide simple security metrics for cloud providers.

We are glad that you are taking a look at our offerings and we hope that our products and services fit your needs or at least make your lives a little easier.
